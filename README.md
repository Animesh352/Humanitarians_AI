## **Developing a Suite of Agentic AI Tools**  

We are building a **comprehensive library of agentic AI tools** to enable AI systems to take **purposeful actions** in the world.  

### **How Does Agentic AI Work?**  
Agentic AI follows a **four-step process**:  

1. **Perceive** ‚Äì AI gathers and processes **data** from various sources (e.g., sensors, databases, digital interfaces).  
2. **Reason** ‚Äì A **Large Language Model (LLM)** serves as the **orchestrator**, generating solutions, accessing **proprietary data sources**, and coordinating specialized AI models.  
3. **Act** ‚Äì The AI **executes tasks** through integrations with external tools via **APIs**, while built-in **guardrails** ensure safe execution.  
4. **Learn** ‚Äì A continuous **feedback loop** enhances AI efficiency, improving decision-making and operational effectiveness.

### **Skills Mapping: AI4ED Lex ‚Äì Bridging Job Market Demands with Education**  
üîó [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/nikbearbrown/Skills-Mapping)  

**AI4ED Lex** is an **open-source lexical database** designed to **map skills from job listings and educational curricula** using **natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and statistical modeling**. This project enhances **workforce alignment, career planning, and curriculum development** by systematically identifying and categorizing employment-related terms.  

---

## **Key Features & Methodologies**  

### **Advanced Skills Extraction & Classification**  
- **Automated Skill Extraction** ‚Äì Uses **Poisson distribution-based Discriminating Terms analysis** to extract key job-related skills from **job postings and course syllabi**.  
- **Semantic Enrichment** ‚Äì Enhances **word associations** with **synonyms, acronyms, spelling variants, and job classifications (Skills, Certifications, Job Titles, Sectors, and Traits)**.  
- **Contextual Rolling Window Classification (CRWC)** ‚Äì Classifies skills based on contextual word proximity for **accurate industry tagging**.  
- **LLM-Powered Accuracy Boost** ‚Äì Uses **Large Language Models (LLMs)** to verify and enrich missing skill data.  

### **Comparative Analysis & Workforce Development**  
- **Alignment with Existing Frameworks** ‚Äì Benchmarks AI4ED Lex against:  
  - **ESCO (European Skills Framework)**  
  - **O*NET (U.S. Job Database)**  
  - **WGU Skills Library**  
  - **Commercial Skill Mapping Tools**  
- **Job Market Integration** ‚Äì Improves workforce readiness by ensuring **curriculum-to-career alignment** with real-world job demands.  
- **Industry-Specific Acronym Detection** ‚Äì Identifies acronyms using custom **heuristics for better skills classification**.  

### **Applications of AI4ED Lex**  
- **Educational Program Enhancement** ‚Äì Helps institutions tailor curricula to meet in-demand job skills.  
- **Career Pathway Optimization** ‚Äì Guides students and professionals toward relevant career opportunities.  
- **Labor Market Transparency** ‚Äì Bridges the gap between **educational content and industry requirements**.  
- **AI-Powered Resume & Job Matching** ‚Äì Matches skills in **resumes and job postings** for **optimized hiring processes**.  

---

## **Innovative Technologies Used**  
- **Poisson Distribution Analysis** ‚Äì Identifies key **employment-related terms** by comparing job descriptions with Wikipedia data.  
- **NLP Tools: NLTK, spaCy, PyDictionary** ‚Äì Enhances **semantic tagging and synonym recognition**.  
- **Machine Learning-Based Skill Categorization** ‚Äì Uses **rolling word windows and statistical tests** to classify terms.  
- **LLM Integration for Continuous Improvement** ‚Äì Enhances **skill mapping precision** and **fills missing skill data dynamically**.  

AI4ED Lex provides a **scalable, adaptive, and open-source solution** to skill mapping, ensuring **education and workforce training stay relevant** in an evolving job market.
